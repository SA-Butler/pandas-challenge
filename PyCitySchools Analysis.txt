PyCitySchools Analysis

Conclusions from the Data Derived:

1. Charter Schools have a higher % Overall Pass rate than the District Schools. 
2. District schools tend to be greater in size than Charter schools
3. The % Passing Math for the District Schools is significantly lower than the Charter Schools. 
4. The budget per student figures do not indicate a trend with higher the budget, the better the overall % passing. Therefore indicating that purely monetary factors are not the most critical in success.
